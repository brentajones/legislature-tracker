Missouri Legislature Tracker

Welcome to St. Louis Public Radio and the Beacon's Missouri Legislature Tracker.

We've selected several bills to keep an eye on in the coming months. You can choose a category below, or click "Recent" to see the bills we're tracking that have had some action in the past 7 days.

Once you've chosen an area, you'll be able to see a list of bills and summaries. You can also click "More Detail" on any bill to see recent actions, sponsors and links to the bill on Missouri government Web sites.
